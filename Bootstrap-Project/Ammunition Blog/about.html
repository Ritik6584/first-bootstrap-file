Augmented Reality (AR) guns merge virtual and physical worlds, offering immersive gaming experiences. They utilize AR technology to overlay digital elements onto the real environment, enhancing gameplay. These guns often come with sensors and tracking systems to accurately detect movements and interactions, providing realistic feedback to users. AR guns are popular in gaming, training simulations, and recreational activities, offering a unique blend of digital entertainment and physical engagement. Fighter jets are high-performance military aircraft designed for air-to-air combat. They possess exceptional speed, maneuverability, and firepower, often featuring advanced avionics and weaponry systems. Used by air forces worldwide, fighter jets play a crucial role in defending airspace, conducting reconnaissance missions, and providing close air support. They come in various models and configurations, ranging from lightweight, agile fighters for dogfighting to multirole jets capable of engaging both air and ground targets. Fighter jets undergo continuous development to maintain superiority in modern aerial warfare
